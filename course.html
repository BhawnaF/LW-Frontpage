Complete guide to AI - Training Course

Before diving into AI, it's essential to understand what AI is and its various subfields. AI is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. AI encompasses several subdomains, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics.
